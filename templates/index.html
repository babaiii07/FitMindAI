💡 Lifestyle Tips

Sleep Quality

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night. Your muscles grow and recover while you sleep. Keep a consistent sleep schedule even on weekends.

Hydration

Drink at least 3L of water daily. Carry a water bottle with you and sip throughout the day. Proper hydration supports metabolism and recovery.

Progress Tracking

Take weekly progress photos and measurements. The scale doesn't tell the whole story - focus on strength gains, measurements, and how clothes fit.

Consistency

Results come from consistent effort over time. Stick to the plan for at least 8 weeks before making significant changes. Small daily habits lead to big changes.
